Gameblyo

Overview

Gameblyo offers personalized vision therapy for amblyopia using web/mobile apps, red-cyan glasses, and a tracking platform. The platform monitors progress, allowing adaptable therapy. Red-cyan glasses split images for each eye, stimulating them differently.

This method enhances individual eye stimuli and trains the brain for synchronized use of both eyes.

Problem

What is Amblyopia?

Amblyopia aka Lazy Eye is an eye condition when the vision of one of your eyes doesnâ€™t develop the way it should. Without treatment, your brain will learn to ignore the image that comes from the weaker eye.

Target Audience

People suffering from amblyopia, mostly children form 3 to 12 years old

Problem statements

  • The need to wear an eye patch for too long time. (avg. 4-6 hrs per day)
  • The eye patch causes discomfort, allergic reactions, psychological issues.
  • Misunderstanding between a child and parents.
  • General vision is neither formed nor restored (binocular vision)
